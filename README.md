## 📊 Key Statistics & Impact

### **HCV Burden in Cox's Bazar**
- **86,000** adults living with chronic HCV infection
- **~1 in 5** adults prevalence
- **95%+** unaware of their infection
- **0%** previously had access to treatment

### **Program Goals (2026)**
- 🎯 **90%** population screened
- 🎯 **80%** of HCV-infected identified
- 🎯 **8,400** patients treated
- 🎯 **95%** cure rates (SVR12)
- 🎯 **50%** reduction in HCV-related morbidity

### **CVD Integration**
- 🎯 **20-25%** HTN diagnosis in screened population
- 🎯 **80%** at BP target within 6 months
- 🎯 **10-15%** diabetes diagnosis
- 🎯 **75%** at glucose target within 6 months

## 💡 Key Innovation Features

### **1. Decentralization**
- 🏘️ Services delivered in communities, not distant hospitals
- 📍 Eliminates movement restrictions & travel barriers
- 🚶 Walking distance to care (same camp, same block)

### **2. Task-Shifting**
- 👥 Non-specialists deliver 90%+ of care
- 💪 CHWs → screening & adherence support
- 🏥 Nurses/MAs → treatment management
- 👨‍⚕️ Physicians → complex cases only

### **3. Integration**
- 🔗 HCV & CVD screened together (same visit)
- 📋 One registration, unified follow-up
- ⏰ Reduced clinic visits & patient burden

### **4. Patient-Centered**
- ⏱️ Same-day testing & treatment available
- 📍 All care at one location
- 💰 100% free services
- 🌐 Community languages

### **5. Evidence-Based**
- 🔬 WHO-endorsed algorithms
- 📚 Latest clinical evidence
- 🎯 Validated assessment tools (APRI, CVD risk)
- 📊 Real-time quality monitoring
