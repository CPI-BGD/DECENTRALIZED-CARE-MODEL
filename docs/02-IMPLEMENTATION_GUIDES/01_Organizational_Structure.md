# Organizational Structure & Governance

**Version 3.0 | February 2026**

## Program Governance

### Central Coordination Unit (CPI Headquarters)
- **Director**: Health Program Manager (MOHAMMAD ARIFUL ISLAM)
- **Responsibilities**: Overall program direction, donor relations, policy coordination
- **Team**: Program Officer, Finance Officer, M&E Specialist

### Camp-Level Implementation Teams

#### Camp 1W Team
- **Camp Coordinator**: Oversees all activities in Camp 1W
- **Clinical Lead**: Supervises clinical protocols and staff
- **CHW Supervisor**: Manages 30-40 CHWs
- **Data Manager**: Oversees data collection and reporting
- **Support Staff**: Drivers, cleaners, security

#### Camp 04 Team
- **Camp Coordinator**: Oversees all activities in Camp 04
- **Clinical Lead**: Supervises clinical protocols and staff
- **CHW Supervisor**: Manages 25-30 CHWs
- **Data Manager**: Oversees data collection and reporting
- **Support Staff**: Drivers, cleaners, security

#### Camp 05 Team (RTMI-led with CPI Support)
- **RTMI Camp Coordinator**: Direct implementation
- **CPI HSS Officer**: Capacity building and support
- **Clinical Team**: Nurses, medical officers
- **CHW Team**: 20-25 CHWs

## Partnership Coordination

### icddr,b Research Team
- **Research Director**: Overall research coordination
- **Field Coordinator**: On-site research activities
- **Data Analyst**: RCT data management
- **Responsibilities**: Care model evaluation, RCT implementation, evidence generation

### WHO Technical Support
- **Country Representative**: Policy coordination
- **Technical Officer**: Training and supervision
- **Quality Assurance Officer**: Protocol adherence monitoring

### Ministry of Health
- **District Health Officer**: Policy integration
- **Program Officer**: Government coordination
- **Responsibilities**: Policy endorsement, government linkages, scale-up planning

## Decision-Making Processes

### Program Steering Committee
- **Meets**: Monthly
- **Members**: CPI Director, Camp Coordinators, icddr,b Representative, WHO Officer, MoH Representative
- **Function**: Strategic decisions, problem-solving, policy alignment

### Technical Working Group
- **Meets**: Bi-weekly
- **Members**: Clinical Leads, Data Managers, Training Coordinator
- **Function**: Protocol refinement, quality assurance, technical troubleshooting

### Camp-Level Meetings
- **Frequency**: Weekly
- **Participants**: All camp staff
- **Function**: Operational updates, problem-solving, team coordination
