# Implementation Roadmap & Timeline

**Version 3.0 | February 2026**

## Phase 1: Preparation & Capacity Building (Months 1-3)

### Month 1: Planning & Setup
- Finalize implementation agreements with partners
- Recruit and onboard staff
- Establish office infrastructure
- Conduct baseline surveys
- **Target**: 100% recruitment, baseline data collected

### Month 2: Training & Mobilization
- Conduct CHW training (2-week module)
- Conduct nurse training (3-week module)
- Conduct medical officer training (4-week module)
- Community mobilization and awareness
- **Target**: 100% of staff trained, 80% community awareness

### Month 3: Pilot & Refinement
- Pilot screening in 2-3 blocks
- Test protocols and workflows
- Refine based on pilot feedback
- Establish data collection systems
- **Target**: 5,000 screened, protocols refined

## Phase 2: Scale-Up (Months 4-9)

### Months 4-6: Camp 1W & 04 Expansion
- Expand screening to all blocks in Camps 1W & 04
- Initiate treatment for identified cases
- Establish regular clinic schedules
- **Target**: 50,000 screened, 2,000 treated

### Months 7-9: Camp 05 Integration
- Expand to Camp 05 via RTMI
- Integrate with existing RTMI services
- Establish partnership protocols
- **Target**: 30,000 screened, 2,000 treated

## Phase 3: Consolidation (Months 10-12)

### Months 10-12: Optimization & Sustainability
- Optimize workflows based on experience
- Strengthen supervision and quality assurance
- Establish sustainability mechanisms
- Plan for 2027 continuation
- **Target**: 8,400 total treated, 95%+ cure rates
